# Support

BlueTeam.News is released as-is, without a support commitment, service-level
agreement, response-time guarantee, maintenance schedule, or public roadmap.

Forking and self-hosted use are welcome under the [MIT License](LICENSE). A
focused bug report may be opened for a reproducible defect, but reports may be
closed without action and there is no promise that a fix or update will be
released. Feature requests and unsolicited pull requests are not accepted.

Security vulnerabilities must be reported privately through the process in
[SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md), never through a public issue.
